Welcome to Dinagat Islands

It's time to start your adventure

Far beyond the typical tourist trails, nestled in the northeastern tip of Mindanao, lies the enchanting Dinagat Islands. This is a province of rugged skylines and untouched sanctuaries, where the mountains are crowned with bonsai forests and the shores are guarded by towering limestone formations. Here, the rhythm of the tides dictates the pace of life, and the crystal-clear waters of the Blue Lagoon offer a sanctuary for the soul.

Hidden between the jagged cliffs of Basilisa and the serene sands of Libjo, there exists a paradise that feels frozen in time. Unlike the crowded hubs of the world, Dinagat remains a raw, majestic frontier. The gentle waves of the Pacific wash against the coast of the "Mystical Island," bringing with them the legends of the Ecleo heritage and the vibrant culture of its people. Every sunrise over the Philippine Sea supplies the islands with a golden glow, inviting you to lose yourself in a landscape where nature still reigns supreme.
